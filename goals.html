This is a list of the BGS project goals:

1. Build a Thriving Community: We aim to grow our community to 10,000 members by the end of 2025. We envision a vibrant network of passionate individuals who come together to support, inspire, and collaborate.

2. Engage and Entertain: We’re dedicated to achieving 100,000 active players in our fangame. This goal reflects our commitment to creating a fun, engaging, and high-quality experience that resonates with a wide audience.

3. Empower Through Learning: Our mission includes teaching new skills and fostering personal growth. We plan to offer valuable resources, workshops, and mentorship opportunities to help our members develop and refine their talents.

4. Cultivate Innovation: We aim to be a hub of creativity and innovation, where new ideas are encouraged and brought to life. Our goal is to support groundbreaking projects and initiatives that push boundaries and set new standards.

5. Strengthen Connections: We seek to forge deep, meaningful connections among our members. By organizing events, discussions, and collaborative projects, we’ll create an environment where everyone feels valued and engaged.

6. Promote Inclusivity and Diversity: We are committed to creating an inclusive space where people from all backgrounds feel welcome. Our goal is to celebrate diversity and ensure that everyone has a voice and a place within our community.
